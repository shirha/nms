Note:
Doriguc VII, a Tier 3 (Flourishing) Manufacturing economy (Construction) , has the best Trade Route leg in Omega with a Sell route that is known.
    You can make 2.5M units by purchasing all the Tier 5 (Vector Compressors) and Tier 4 (Holographic Crankshafts) local Trade Goods for 11.7M units and selling them to Toyabe IX, a Power Generation economy (Engineering)
    Even though Mazuna is also a Tier 3 economy (Affluent), there is no known Sell route for their Trade Goods. Use an Economy Scanner to find a Tier 3 Power Generation and Trading economy and improve the rest.
    Space Stations have poor buy % but Freighters may be as good as Trade Outposts and easier to setup. You could probably do the whole Trade Route from a Freighter! The Galactic Trade Terminal inventory changes with each jump.
